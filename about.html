Production optimization

SLB assists clients in optimizing production from oil and gas reservoirs through various techniques, such as artificial lift systems, well stimulation, and production enhancement technologies.

Careers

We are a global technology company driving energy innovation for a balanced planet. So we're looking for talented, driven people committed to innovation and success and who act with integrity.

Well testing

SLB conducts well testing services to evaluate the performance of oil and gas wells, including flow rates, pressure, and fluid properties, to optimize production and reservoir management.

Directional drilling

SLB offers directional drilling services, which involve controlling the trajectory of a wellbore to reach specific targets, maximize reservoir exposure, and enhance production efficiency.

Formation evaluation

SLB provides formation evaluation services to assess the properties of subsurface formations, including porosity, permeability, and fluid content, crucial for determining reservoir characteristics.

Seismic data processing

SLB offers seismic data processing services, which involve analyzing seismic data to understand subsurface structures and identify potential oil and gas reservoirs.
