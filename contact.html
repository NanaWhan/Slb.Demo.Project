Well testing

Schlumberger conducts well testing services to evaluate the performance of oil and gas wells, including flow rates, pressure, and fluid properties, to optimize production and reservoir management.

Directional drilling

Schlumberger offers directional drilling services, which involve controlling the trajectory of a wellbore to reach specific targets, maximize reservoir exposure, and enhance production efficiency.

Formation evaluation

Schlumberger provides formation evaluation services to assess the properties of subsurface formations, including porosity, permeability, and fluid content, crucial for determining reservoir characteristics.

Seismic data processing

Schlumberger offers seismic data processing services, which involve analyzing seismic data to understand subsurface structures and identify potential oil and gas reservoirs.
